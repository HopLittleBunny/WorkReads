# WorkReads – Help & Support

Welcome to **WorkReads**.

WorkReads helps professionals discover high-quality and trending articles that help them get ahead in their career. Currently focused on HR and Change Management, with plans to expand to other functions. The app provides insights related to work, careers, leadership, and personal growth — curated and personalized using AI.

## How WorkReads Works

- Discover curated articles related to work and professional growth through an intuitive swipeable card interface
- Swipe right to save articles, swipe left to dismiss, or super-like to highlight must-reads for the community
- Browse by topic: HR, Change, AI, Future of Work, Leadership, Strategy, Culture, Analytics, Technology, and Talent
- Filter articles by reading time: Quick Reads (5 min), Deep Dives (10-20 min), or Strategy (20+ min)
- Save articles to collections and track your reading progress
- Get personalized recommendations based on your role, interests, and reading preferences

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an account?
No account is required. Your preferences, saved articles, and reading history are stored locally on your device. Optional account creation is available if you want to sync across devices in future updates.

### Is WorkReads free?
Yes, WorkReads is completely free to use.

### How are recommendations generated?
Recommendations are generated based on your selected role (e.g., HR Business Partner, Change & Transformation), interest areas (e.g., Talent Management, People Analytics), and preferred content depth. The app uses an algorithm that considers content quality, recency, source reputation, and community engagement to surface the most relevant articles.

### What data is stored on my device?
- Your role and interest preferences
- Saved and dismissed articles
- Reading progress and streaks
- Custom collections
- Weekly digest preferences

### How do I delete my data?
You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing **founder@hoplittlebunny.com**. To clear local data, you can uninstall and reinstall the app.
